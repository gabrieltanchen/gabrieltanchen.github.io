Hello world, my name is

Gabriel Tan-Chen.

I am a husband, father, and software developer based in Waterloo, Ontario.

I currently work at the Centre for International Governance Innovation, where I help build digital interactives to showcase its research initiatives. I find enjoyment in building a relational experience with the pixels that surround us, as it’s the stories told by those pixels that can influence the reality of tomorrow. Prior to moving to Waterloo, I was a consultant in Toronto working with enterprise companies in industries ranging from banking to manufacturing. I am a graduate of the University of Toronto with an Honours degree in computer science.

When I am unplugged, I am mostly outdoors with my family – probably doing our best to tire out our dog, Luna, though we’ve accepted this may be impossible. I can also be found outside training for and racing in any number of triathlon events or marathons. I believe it’s important to keep the body as active as the mind – and no, all this running still does not manage to exhaust Luna.
